## Overview

IoT and smart home services enable you to:

- **Home Automation**: Control lights, thermostats, locks, and more
- **Device Integration**: Connect Zigbee, Z-Wave, WiFi, and Bluetooth devices
- **Automation Rules**: Create complex automation scenarios
- **Energy Monitoring**: Track and optimize energy consumption
- **Security**: Monitor cameras, sensors, and alarms

## Suggested Services

Additional IoT platforms to consider:

- **OpenHAB** - Vendor-neutral home automation
- **Domoticz** - Lightweight home automation
- **Node-RED** - Flow-based programming for IoT
- **Mosquitto** - MQTT message broker
- **Zigbee2MQTT** - Zigbee to MQTT bridge
