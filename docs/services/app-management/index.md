# App & Server Management

This section covers content management systems and business applications.

## Overview

Application management includes:

- **CMS**: Content management systems
- **ERP**: Enterprise resource planning
- **E-commerce**: Online store platforms
- **Business Apps**: Productivity and collaboration

## Suggested Services

Platforms to consider:

### Content Management
- **WordPress** - Popular CMS
- **Drupal** - Enterprise CMS
- **Ghost** - Publishing platform
- **Strapi** - Headless CMS

### Business Applications
- **Odoo** - Open source ERP
- **ERPNext** - Modern ERP
- **Dolibarr** - ERP/CRM suite
